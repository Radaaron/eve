V. Overall Rating


The overall evaluation requires rating the employee on a scale of 1-3. In making ratings, please refer to your skills evaluation on the previous page.

The rating of a “2” indicates a level of performance expected of a Navitaire employee.

1= Outstanding for Navitaire employees- uniformly excellent performance. A level of performance substantially above the normal competent performance for Navitaire employees. Skills evaluated generally rated at “exceeds expectations.” 2= The “norm” for Navitaire employees- competent performance. Skills evaluated generally rated at “meets expectations.” 3= Below average for Navitaire employees. Skills evaluated generally rated at “needs improvement.”
